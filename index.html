Instructions:

Card Screen: Click on cards to select them. You can check cards by hovering on the cards icon (top-right-corner). After selecting 3 cards a "Start Game" button will appear. Click it to start the game.

Game Screen: Click on tiles to select them, selected tiles appear on lower bar and can be removed by clicking them there. You can navigate with arrow keys. You can see selected cards by hovering on the cards icon (top-right-corner). After selecting 14 tiles a submit button will appear. Click it to finish. (Game ends automatically in 60 seconds).
